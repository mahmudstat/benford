Abdullah Al Mahmud is a lecturer in statistics at Pabna Cadet College, Pabna, where he joined on 19 October, 2019. Before joining there, he was employed as a research assistant at Engineers and Advisors Ltd. (EAL), Dhaka. He also worked as a science contributor for The Daily Prothom Alo. He has written three science books, namely the translation of A Briefer History of Time, Mohabishwer Simana, and Osim Somikoron, and contributed to another multi-authored book, Chondrojoyer 50 Bochor. 

Hailing from Lakshmipur Sadar Upazila, he graduated with B.S. and M.S. degrees from the University of Dhaka.
He founded Bishwo.com and serves as the editor and contributor of মহাবিশ্ব and Stat Mania.
He is very passionate about teaching, writing, programming, and data analysis, and he continues doing all of them.
In my free time, he loves to roam around, connect with people, and watch sky.
He is married to Salma Siddika, also an alumni of University of Dhaka.
